## Project Overview
This HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce composition, promotion eligibility, retirement outlook, and employee distribution. It is designed to support HR teams and management in monitoring key workforce metrics and making data-driven decisions.

The dashboard allows dynamic filtering to analyze different employee segments and scenarios.

---

## Business Objectives
- Track total and active employees
- Monitor employees due and not due for promotion
- Identify employees nearing retirement
- Analyze workforce distribution by job level and service years
- Understand employee distance from office

---

## Key KPIs Displayed
- **Total Employees**
- **Active Workers**
- **Male vs Female Distribution**
- **Employees Due for Promotion**
- **Employees Not Due for Promotion**
- **Upcoming Retirements**
- **Service Years Distribution**
- **Employees by Job Level**
- **Distance from Office**

---

## Dashboard Insights
- The majority of employees are currently **not due for promotion**, indicating workforce stability.
- **Lower and mid-level job roles** form the largest portion of the organization.
- Only a **small percentage of employees are approaching retirement**, reducing short-term attrition risk.
- Most employees live **very close to the office**, which minimizes commute-related productivity issues.
- Gender distribution remains relatively balanced across different filtered views.
